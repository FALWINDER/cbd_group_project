About CBD Group

CBD Group is a collaborative team of developers dedicated to building digital learning platforms that support school students in exploring subjects like Art, Mathematics, and Technology. Our goal is to make learning more engaging, accessible, and interactive.

This platform was developed using an Agile methodology, working closely with educators and clients to refine features and provide the best experience for students, tutors, and administrators. It includes curated articles, biographies, and references to key events, people, and concepts in each subject.

Who Can Use This Platform?
  • Students: Can browse and search articles by category or keyword.
  • Tutors: Can add and update articles relevant to their subject areas.
  • Administrators: Can manage all content and users on the platform.

This project is part of an initiative to enhance digital education using modern tools such as Django, Bootstrap, and responsive web design. We are continuously improving based on user feedback.
